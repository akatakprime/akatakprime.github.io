Controls:
W = up | A = left | S = down | D = right
To use an item, click on it or type in its number. For numbers with multiple digits, hold space and type in the number.
Monster:
Creature that will hunt and kill you. You can stun it with a knife.
Prisoner:
Man who needs to be rescued. He will follow you unless attacked by a monster.
Knife:
If the bearer and the monster collide at the end of the turn, it will stun the monster for 3 turns. Can be handed to a prisoner.
Torch:
Will light the space around the bearer for 20 turns. Can be handed to a prisoner. Can be accumulated.
Inventory:
You will begin each level with the items left over from the last successful level, so use your inventory strategically.
